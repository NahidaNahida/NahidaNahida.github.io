    Yuechen Li

    Yuechen Li (李玥辰)

    GitHub | Google Scholar | ORCID

    Yuechen is a four-year Ph.D. candidate with Beihang University. His interests include both theoretical and empirical research on foundations of quantum software engineering (QSE), and applications of quantum information theory /quantum computing. The current studies during his Ph.D. program have a focus on quantum software testing.

    Bulletin
    (2026.3) A new study led by Yuechen, A Dynamic Test Oracle for Quantum Programs with Separable Output States, has been accepted for publication in IEEE Transactions on Software Engineering (TSE). This study systematically investigates the test oracle problem for quantum programs, and provides a feasible solution for handling curse of dimensionality in quantum software testing.
